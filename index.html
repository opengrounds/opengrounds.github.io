what is open:grounds?

a constellation of maps for the things we share.

open:grounds started with one question: how do we map what is free, public, and shared in a town that has everything but can't communicate it on an accessible scale? this project focused on davis, california looking at the multiple layers of community ownership on the levels of free to low-cost. the goal is to expand this project to other communities, and in other formats outside of the digital realm!

if you have questions or would like to contribute, please reach out by messaging picklingbeets.bsky.social!
